Madagascar day gecko

A bright and unusual reptile with unique features

An amazing representative of the fauna of Madagascar

About the view

The Madagascar day gecko (Phelsuma madagascariensis) is a colorful lizard of the gecko family. It has a rich green color with red spots on its back.

It reaches a length of up to 22-25 cm. It is active during the day, unlike most other geckos. It has prehensile paws with microscopic hairs that allow it to move on vertical surfaces.

Living environment

It is endemic to Madagascar and lives in the northern and eastern parts of the island. Prefers tropical forests, often inhabits trees and palm trees.

Recently, he has successfully adapted to life in urban environments. The optimal temperature for life is 25-28°C during the day and 20-22°C at night.

Features

It feeds on insects, fruits and nectar. It is an important pollinator of some Madagascar plants.

In captivity, with proper care, they can live up to 15-20 years. They require special conditions of detention with maintaining humidity and temperature.
